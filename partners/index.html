Join the Teamboarding Partner Program

Teamboarding helps companies optimize the onboarding of new talent in technical teams. Through our partner program, consultants, software companies and talent experts can offer an innovative solution while unlocking new business opportunities.

Become a Partner

Why become a Teamboarding partner?

By joining our network, you will:

EXPAND YOUR OFFERING

Broaden Your Service Portfolio with Specialized Onboarding.

Enhance your existing services by incorporating a cutting-edge onboarding solution. This strategic addition allows you to cater to a wider range of client needs and position your business as a comprehensive solution provider. Offer a more holistic approach to client success and deepen your client relationships.

GENERATE ADDITIONAL REVENUE

Unlock New Revenue Streams Through Onboarding Solutions.

Capitalize on the growing demand for effective onboarding by earning recurring commissions on each implementation. Furthermore, leverage your expertise to provide specialized onboarding consulting, creating additional revenue opportunities and solidifying your position as a trusted advisor.

STAND OUT IN THE MARKET

Differentiate Your Business with Enhanced Technical Onboarding

In today's competitive landscape, offering superior technical onboarding is a key differentiator. Empower your clients to structure and optimize their onboarding processes, resulting in improved client satisfaction, faster time-to-value, and stronger client retention. Position yourself as a leader in client onboarding and gain a significant competitive edge.

WHO IS THIS PROGRAM FOR?

Our program is designed for

Digital transformation consultancies

Optimize enterprise onboarding with cutting-edge technology

Software factories and IT providers

Add value by ensuring the smooth integration of new talent.

Employee experience consultancies

Offer a complete solution to enhance team onboarding.

Onboarding specialists

Expand your impact with a platform designed to improve talent integration.

Benefits & Revenue Model
Commission per Client

Earn a percentage of the software’s value for each implementation.

Additional Services

Offer consulting and charge for implementation, with fees adjusted to project complexity.

Official certification

Become a Certified Teamboarding Implementer and access exclusive resources.
